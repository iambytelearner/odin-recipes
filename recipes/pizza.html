Pizza


Description -

This vegetarian pizza recipe is a lifesaver in those situations. Piled with bell peppers, briny artichokes, red onion, fresh basil, and 3 types of cheese, it tastes like delivery pizza (maybe even better?), and it’s quick and easy to make at home. It’s one of our weeknight staples. I hope you love it too!


Ingredients -

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Onion
  • Black olives
  • Jalapeno pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps -

  1. Stretch the dough neatly in a circular shape
  2. Spread the sauce on the dough. Cover the while dough
  3. Add the cheddar and mozzarella cheese after adding the sauce
  4. Add the selected vegetables like onion, olives, jalapenoes
  5. Finally add the fresh basils on top
  6. Bake the pizza at 500 degree Frenhite for 1 to 13 minutes
